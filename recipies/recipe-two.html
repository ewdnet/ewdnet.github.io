Duvec

one of my favorite recipes because it is just so tasty and at the same time smells wonderful and reminds of the grandparents. Duvec or Djuvec ..

For the Duvec we need: 500g meat, toatoes 500g, red wine, water, rosemary, tomato paste, pepper & salt, peppers 3 pieces, onions 2 big ones, garlic, 2 cup of rice

Ingredients Preparation Quantity
Pork Meat Cut the meat into about 2 cm thick slices and pound them lightly 500g
Onions, Garlic, Tomatoes, Peppers season the meat with garlic powder, salt, pepper and rosemary. Fry or grill briefly in the heated oil on both sides, but do not cook through. Heat oil in a saucepan, add chopped onions, crushed garlic cloves, tomato slices, paprika fruits cut into strips, 1 to 3 hot peppers (depending on heat) and 1 tablespoon tomato paste, season with salt. Steam everything for about 5 minutes 2 pieces, 6 Gloves, 500g, 3 pieces
Rice add the washed rice 2 Cups
Spices add the spieces salt, rosemary, pepper, 1 tablespoon tomato paste
Water & red wine put the Water & Wine in it and cover it. Boil it for 15 minutes. Last Step: Pour the fried or grilled schnitzel on top and steam for another 15 minutes 3/8 l half Water,half red wine
